About Luma Hair and Beauty

Luma Hair and Beauty offers Affordable Luxury in the heart of Nairobi. We specialize in professional make-up, wig styling, high-quality Vietnam wigs, braiding, temporary dreadlocks, twist outs, silk pressing, nail services, hair coloring, relaxing, hydration treatments, and natural hair care.

Our goal is to help every client look good and feel confident. With our team of experienced stylists and beauty experts, we provide personalized services tailored to your unique needs and preferences.

Located at Tom Mboya Street, Platinum Plaza (Opposite Imenti House), 7th Floor, Moi Avenue, Nairobi, we welcome clients from all over Kenya to experience our premium yet affordable beauty services.
